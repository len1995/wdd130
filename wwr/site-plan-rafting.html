Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote tourism, attract the market into rafting sports, and promote a awareness for a healthy and fun recreation.

Audience

Target market: * Any organization or companies who want to have team building, conquering the river with teamwork and beating the fear of dangerous white water. * Families who would like to have fun and get together. * People who loves extreme sport. * Beginners who would like to experience different levels of challenge in water sport.
