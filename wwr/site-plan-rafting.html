Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote tourism, attract the people of all ages into rafting sports, promote awareness of nature in a healthy environment. It develops love for sports, teamwork, cooperation, respect and love for nature.

Audience

Target market:

  • Any organization or companies who want to have team building, conquering the river with teamwork and beating the fear of dangerous white water.
  • Families who would like to have fun and get together.
  • People who loves extreme sport.
  • Beginners who would like to experience different levels of challenge in water sport.
